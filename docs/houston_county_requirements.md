# Houston County, Georgia Eviction Requirements

## Court Information
- **Court**: Houston County Magistrate Court
- **Address**: 202 Carl Vinson Parkway, Warner Robins, GA 31088
- **Phone**: (478) 542-2100
- **Hours**: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday-Friday

## Required Documents
1. **Dispossessory Affidavit** (Form)
2. **Summons** (Issued by court)
3. **Filing Fee**: $81.00 (subject to change)
4. **Service Fee**: $50.00 per defendant (Sheriff's office)

## Georgia-Specific Requirements

### Notice Periods
- **Non-payment**: 7-day demand notice (OCGA ยง 44-7-50)
- **Lease violation**: 30-day notice (if month-to-month)
- **Holdover**: 60-day notice (if no lease)

### Military Considerations
- SCRA (Servicemembers Civil Relief Act) check required
- Additional protections for active duty military

### Filing Process
1. Complete affidavit and summons request
2. Pay filing fee at Magistrate Court clerk's office
3. Clerk issues summons and sets court date
4. Sheriff serves papers (5-7 business days)
5. Court hearing scheduled 7-10 days after service

## Local Rules
- Electronic filing available through nCourt
- Walk-in filings accepted
- Emergency filings handled same-day
- Spanish translation services available
